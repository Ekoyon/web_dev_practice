Moisture

  • Increases heat loss, due to evaporation and superheating of vapour
  • Helps to a limit, in binding fires
  • Aids radiation heat transfer

Sulphur Content

Typical range is 0.5% - 0.8%. Other fuels approximate Sulphur content:

Fuel Oil Percentage of Sulphur
Kerosene 0.05-0.2
Diesel 0.05-0.25
Furnance Oil 2.0-4.0

Effects of Sulphur

  1. Affects clinkering and slagging
  2. Risk of corrosion by suphuric acid formed during and after combustion, and condensing to the cool parts of the chimney
  3. It limits exit flue gas temperature
